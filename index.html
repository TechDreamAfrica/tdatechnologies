Who We Are

TDA Technologies is dedicated to delivering software development, data management and analysis, advertising and marketing, and network systems and security services.

We focus on providing customized solutions—including web, mobile, and desktop applications—for small and medium-sized businesses (SMEs) in Africa. Our vision is to empower businesses through technology, enabling them to digitize their operations, scale effectively, and compete in an increasingly digital economy.
